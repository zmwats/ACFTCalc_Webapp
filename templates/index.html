Calculating your Army Combat Fitness Test (ACFT) score has never been easier. Our user-friendly ACFT Score Calculator takes the guesswork out of your performance assessment. 


How It Works:

Select Your Event: Choose the specific ACFT event you want to calculate your score for. We cover all six events, including the 3-repetition Maximum Deadlift, Standing Power Throw, Hand-Release Push-Up, Sprint-Drag-Carry, Leg Tuck, and Two-Mile Run.

Input Your Results: Enter your performance data for the chosen event. For example, if you're calculating your score for the Two-Mile Run, just enter your completion time.

Get Instant Results: In a matter of seconds, our ACFT Score Calculator will generate your score for the selected event. You'll know exactly where you stand and what you need to work on.
